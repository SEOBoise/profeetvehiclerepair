About Our Company

At Pro Fleet Vehicle Repair, we're dedicated to revolutionizing fleet management through our comprehensive vehicle maintenance and fuel optimization solutions. Located in Saint Clairsville, Ohio, we've established ourselves as a trusted partner for businesses seeking to maximize their fleet efficiency while minimizing operational costs.

Under the leadership of James S. Frigo, our team brings decades of combined experience in fleet management, vehicle maintenance, and fuel optimization. We understand that each fleet faces unique challenges, which is why we develop customized solutions that address your specific needs while maintaining the highest standards of service quality.

Our approach combines cutting-edge technology with proven maintenance strategies to help you achieve optimal fleet performance. We specialize in implementing preventive maintenance programs that extend vehicle life, reduce downtime, and maximize your return on investment. Our fuel management expertise helps you navigate the complexities of fuel costs while implementing strategies that lead to significant savings.

Our Services

Fleet Management Solutions

  • Comprehensive vehicle maintenance programs that keep your fleet running at peak efficiency, including regular inspections, repairs, and performance monitoring.
  • Advanced diagnostic services utilizing state-of-the-art equipment to identify and resolve issues before they become costly problems.
  • Custom maintenance scheduling that aligns with your operational demands while minimizing vehicle downtime.
  • Detailed reporting and analytics to track maintenance history, costs, and vehicle performance metrics.

Fuel Management Expertise

  • Strategic fuel consumption analysis and optimization programs designed to reduce operational costs while maintaining performance.
  • Implementation of advanced fuel monitoring systems to track usage patterns and identify opportunities for improvement.
  • Driver training programs focused on fuel-efficient driving techniques and best practices.
  • Regular fuel system maintenance and optimization to ensure maximum efficiency and minimize waste.
